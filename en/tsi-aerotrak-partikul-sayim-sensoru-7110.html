Technical Details

Particle count data can be easily and continuously transferred to a facility monitoring system—such as TSI’s FMS 5—for secure data capture and analysis. In particular, when integrated with the FMS 5, you gain a powerful productivity tool featuring capabilities such as Statistical Process Control. These particle counters can also store up to 2,000 sample records and provide data redundancy.Model 7110 complies with all the stringent requirements specified in ISO 21501-4. It is calibrated using NIST-traceable PSL spheres in accordance with TSI’s world-class Classifier and Condensation Particle Counters, the recognized standard for particle measurement.
