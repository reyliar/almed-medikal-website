BactoSense CORE

Product Details

Fast
BactoSense CORE detects contamination and microbial changes in real time, enabling immediate intervention.
Prevent risks by proactively addressing anomalies and deviations in microbial profiles at an early stage.
​ It saves time and resources by completing water analysis in just 20 minutes.
Accurate
BactoSense CORE consistently meets the increasingly stringent regulatory standards of the drinking water industry.
Easy to Use
Thanks to its touchscreen, BactoSense CORE does not require an external monitor.
Its user-friendly interface makes it easy to retrieve and analyze water quality data.
​ Step into the digital age of microbiology and experience seamless integration with existing systems such as SCADA.
Fully Automatic The BactoSense CORE’s sampling process is a fully automated procedure from start to finish.
No need for manual colony counting. No user bias. No exposure to risks.

  • GMP-compliant process control

  • Monitoring of biological activity in wastewater treatment processes
  • Control of reclaimed water quality
  • Pre-discharge microbiological assessment
  • Microbiological analysis and validation studies
  • Performance testing of new treatment technologies
