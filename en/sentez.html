Synthesis Process

Synthesis and Process Development Stages

Synthesis, purification, and analysis of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) are supported by GMP-standard production process development and scale-up studies.

Reaction Development

Determining optimum reaction conditions for the target molecule and optimizing yield and selectivity.

Purification

Obtaining high-purity products through methods such as crystallization, distillation, and column chromatography.

Analysis & Verification

Structural characterization and purity determination using HPLC, GC, NMR, FTIR, and mass spectrometry.

Scale-Up

Transition from laboratory scale to pilot production, process validation, and reproducibility studies.

Application Areas

In Which Areas Are Synthesis Studies Conducted?

Pharmaceutical APIs

  • Hemostatic active ingredients and blood-stopping formulations
  • Antiseptic and disinfectant active ingredients
  • API synthesis for sublingual drug formulations
  • Radiation-protective chemical compounds

Industrial Applications

  • Reaction optimization in controlled environment
  • Process development and improvement studies
  • Analytical method development and validation
  • Stability and shelf-life evaluations
Info:Synthesis studies are carried out with reference to international pharmacopoeia standards (USP, EP). All processes are documented in line with GMP principles and supported by quality-control processes.
