Hemostatic Antiseptic Gas Compress

A sterile gauze compress system that combines hemostatic support for bleeding control with hygiene support on the wound surface. It is used by applying direct pressure to the wound and is developed for emergency response and surgical use.

Sterility Sterile impregnated gauze compress
Effect Hemostatic + Antiseptic
Application Direct pressure on the wound
Usage Emergency response and surgery
Product Features

Hemostatic Antiseptic Gauze Compress Details

The sterile gauze compress system is impregnated with hemostatic agents that help control bleeding and also provides hygiene support on the wound surface through its antiseptic properties. It is supplied in single-use sterile packaging.

Hemostatik Effect

  • Formulation that aids rapid bleeding control
  • Active ingredients that support the clotting process
  • Ease of direct application to wound surface
  • Effective use in trauma and surgical scenarios

Antiseptik Protection

  • Hygiene support on wound surface
  • Sterile packed, single-use
  • Helps reduce infection risk
  • Suitable for field and hospital conditions
Areas of Use

When Is It Used?

Emergency Response

  • Initial intervention in trauma-related bleeding
  • Use in field and disaster areas
  • Military and civil defense scenarios
  • Can be included in first aid kits

Klinik Usage

  • Bleeding control in surgical operations
  • Emergency department interventions
  • Wound care and dressing support
  • Post-operative wound management
