ERWEKA AR 403 Pharmaceutical Test Device

ERWEKA AR 403 is a modular all-purpose system that can run multiple attachments with a single motor unit for pharmaceutical, chemical, cosmetic, and food R&D and small-scale production processes. It can be used as a dissolution, disintegration, and friability tester.

Motor Power Three-Phase AC, 750 W
Speed Range 20 – 400 rpm
Torque Value 23 Nm / 30 Nm class
Modular Structure Multiple Attachment Support
Technical Specifications

ERWEKA AR 403 Technical Details

Thanks to its modular structure, different pharmaceutical quality-control tests such as dissolution, disintegration, and friability can be performed with a single motor unit. It is compliant with USP and EP standards.

Which Tests Is It Used For?

Dissolution

  • Dissolution rate determination of tablets and capsules
  • Basket and paddle methods (USP App. 1 & 2)

Disintegration & Friability

  • Tablet disintegration time determination
  • Tablet friability resistance measurement
  • Granule, pellet, and coated tablet testing
