BactoSense PURE

Product Details

Transparent, compliant reporting
BactoSense PURE easily meets legal requirements and maintains water purity standards throughout your operations.
It complies with the FDA’s 21 CFR Part 11, follows international guidelines such as Ph. Eur. 5.1.6, and ensures that electronic records are secure, reliable, and traceable.
Higher efficiency, lower costs
Maximize efficiency by reducing manual controls and interventions in your water management systems.
BactoSense PURE minimizes downtime and associated costs by enabling the timely detection of microbial threats, delivering a smoother, more efficient operation.
Continuous monitoring reduces risks
By continuously assessing water purity, BactoSense PURE enables immediate intervention and helps you maintain your product’s regulatory compliance.
Speed and accuracy
With BactoSense PURE, you obtain fast and accurate microbial counts, automate everything from sample collection to the analysis process, and reduce the need for manual input and the likelihood of errors.
Operational reliability made easy
BactoSense PURE is ideal for critical environments ranging from pharmaceutical manufacturing to semiconductor facilities; it delivers precision, consistent performance, secure data management, an intuitive interface, integration with digital systems, and—most importantly—peace of mind.
