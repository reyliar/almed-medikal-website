CBRN Defense Equipment

CBRN Sampling Kit, CBRN NBC Equipment, decontamination systems, chemical and biological detection devices. NATO-standard, field-tested products.

  • CBRN Sampling Kit: Biological and chemical sampling from air, water, soil, and surfaces. NATO stock numbered.
  • GDA-P Personal Detector: IMS + PID/ECD technology, CWA and TIC detection, IP65 protection.
  • CBRN First Aid Kit: Fullers Earth-based, 6-component decontamination kit.
  • Decontamination Powder: NBC cleaning agent with high chemical absorption capacity.

First Aid & Emergency Response

Individual first aid kits (IFAK), CBRN first aid kits, burn treatment supplies, tourniquet systems. Suitable for military and civilian use.

  • VANGUARD IFAK: 19-piece individual first aid kit. Includes tourniquet, NPA, hemostatic dressing.
  • Automatic Tourniquet: Provides fast and reliable bleeding control in emergencies.
  • Burn Treatment Supplies: Specially formulated burn dressing sets and emergency response equipment.

Pharmaceutical Raw Materials & Production

Pharmaceutical raw materials, synthesis studies, gas compress systems, hemostatic products, pharmaceutical testing devices, and laboratory equipment.

  • ERWEKA AR 403: Modular pharmaceutical testing device. R&D and quality control with dissolution tester feature.
  • MEDMAK Machine: LXZ-50/75/100 series swab folding and packaging system.
  • Hemostatic Gas Compress: Medical gas compress system with antiseptic and hemostatic properties.

R&D & Advanced Technology Projects

Hemostatic drug formulations, LED operating room lamp, automatic tourniquet, radiation protection products, and mobile CBRN laboratory projects.

  • Hemostatic Drugs: Innovative hemostatic formulations with rapid clotting effect.
  • Radiation Protection: Chemical compound-based radiation isolation and protective equipment.
  • Mobile CBRN Laboratory: Fully equipped, on-site bio-chem-rad detection and analysis.

Which Fields Do We Work In?

  • Pharmaceutical Raw Materials
    Synthesis, purification, and analysis of active pharmaceutical ingredients (API). Development of production processes at GMP standards. Custom formulation studies and scale-up services.
  • Pharma Production Systems & Equipment
    Tablet pressing, coating, bottling, and packaging lines. ERWEKA pharmaceutical testing devices, MEDMAK swab packaging systems, and fully automated production solutions.
  • Gas Compress System & Equipment
    Hemostatic antiseptic gas compress systems. Medical gas mixtures and distribution equipment. Sterilization and disinfection solutions.
  • Laboratory Systems & Equipment
    Fully equipped systems for R&D laboratories. Analytical instruments, test equipment, consumables, and laboratory setup consulting.
  • Burn Treatment Medical Equipment
    Specially formulated burn dressing sets. Hydrogel-based wound dressings, emergency response kits, and innovative medical products for burn treatment.
  • CBRN Mobile Laboratory
    Fully equipped mobile platform capable of on-site biological, chemical, and radiological detection and analysis. NATO-standard configuration options.
  • Radiation Protection Isolation
    Innovative chemical compound-based radiation shield. High absorption capacity, lightweight and durable structure. Optimized for military and civilian use.
  • Radiation Protection Products
    Personal radiation protective equipment, lead aprons, thyroid protectors, gonadal shields, and radiation barriers. For medical and industrial use.
  • Automatic Tourniquet
    Pneumatic tourniquet systems providing fast and reliable bleeding control in emergencies. One-hand operation, adjustable pressure, and safety mechanisms.
  • LED Operating Room Lamp
    High brightness, low energy consumption, and long-lasting LED technology. Shadowless illumination, adjustable color temperature, and sterile use features.
