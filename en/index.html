KBRN Savunma Ekipmanları

CBRN Sampling Kit, CBRN NBC Equipment, decontamination systems, chemical and biological detection devices. NATO-standard, field-tested products.

  • KBRN Numune Alma Kiti: Biological and chemical sampling from air, water, soil, and surfaces. NATO stock numbered.
  • GDA-P Kişisel Dedektör: IMS + PID/ECD technology, CWA and TIC detection, IP65 protection.
  • KBRN İlk Yardım Çantası: Fullers Earth-based, 6-component decontamination kit.
  • Dekontaminasyon Tozu: NBC cleaning agent with high chemical absorption capacity.

İlk Yardım ve Acil Müdahale

Individual first aid kits (IFAK), CBRN first aid kits, burn treatment supplies, tourniquet systems. Suitable for military and civilian use.

  • VANGUARD IFAK: 19-piece individual first aid kit. Includes tourniquet, NPA, hemostatic dressing.
  • Automatic Tourniquet: Provides fast and reliable bleeding control in emergencies.
  • Yanık Tedavi Malzemeleri: Specially formulated burn dressing sets and emergency response equipment.

İlaç Hammaddeleri ve Üretim

Pharmaceutical raw materials, synthesis studies, gas compress systems, hemostatic products, pharmaceutical testing devices, and laboratory equipment.

  • ERWEKA AR 403: Modular pharmaceutical testing device. R&D and quality control with dissolution tester feature.
  • MEDMAK Makine: LXZ-50/75/100 series swab folding and packaging system.
  • Hemostatik Gaz Kompres: Medical gas compress system with antiseptic and hemostatic properties.

Ar-Ge ve İleri Teknoloji Projeleri

Hemostatic drug formulations, LED operating room lamp, automatic tourniquet, radiation protection products, and mobile CBRN laboratory projects.

  • Kan Durdurucu İlaçlar: Innovative hemostatic formulations with rapid clotting effect.
  • Radyasyon Koruma: Chemical compound-based radiation isolation and protective equipment.
  • Mobil KBRN Laboratuvarı: Fully equipped, on-site bio-chem-rad detection and analysis.
