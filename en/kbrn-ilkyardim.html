CBRN First Aid Kit

A professional kit prepared to meet first-response teams' transport, personal protection, surface decontamination, eye/nasal cleaning, drying, and basic medical supply needs in a single set for chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear field risks.

Operational Scope

Decontamination, Protection & Medical Supplies in One Case

The product set includes a durable carrying case, Fullers Earth-based absorbent/decontamination products, disinfectant and surface cleaning solutions, eye/nasal cleaning tubes, sterile supplies, brush, gloves, and an FFP2 mask.

Transport & Storage

The IP65 polypropylene case is specified for field conditions and provides protection against impact, dust, and water spray.

Decontamination

The powdered decontamination glove, 150 ml Fullers Earth bottle, and chemical/radiological decontamination wipes support surface, equipment, and personal-use scenarios.

Personal Protection

Supplies such as vinyl gloves, FFP2 NR D valved mask, sterile gauze compress, and disposable towel complete the post-intervention cleaning and protection setup.

Item 01

Decontamination Kit Carry Case

The impact-resistant hard polypropylene carrying case is designed to protect costly and sensitive equipment in demanding use areas such as field, police, military, exploration, and scientific research.

  • Inner dimensions: 335 x 250 x 122 mm
  • External dimensions: 340 x 275 x 124 mm
  • Material: PP, polypropylene injection body

Item 02

Chemical and Radioactive Decontamination Powder Glove

The individual decontamination glove with powder inside a polyethylene-backed pad is worn over the user's existing protective glove and enables rapid powder application to equipment and surfaces.

  • Lightweight, single-pack, ready-to-use form
  • Portable small form factor
  • Safe application over liquid contamination
  • Surface decontamination capacity: up to 1 m²
  • Cleaning capacity calculation: 10 g/m² soil/contamination level
  • Suitable for military, emergency response teams, and field personnel
Item 03

150 ml Fullers Earth Decontamination Powder

Modified Fullers Earth with a natural sedimentary clay structure is used for physical absorption of agents on surfaces, skin, clothing, and footwear. As a basic component of military equipment, it can be used for personal protection in field CBRN incidents.

  • Contents: 150 ml Fullers Earth
  • Composition: alumina, silica, iron oxides, lime, magnesia, and water
  • 100% natural Fullers Earth-based
  • High chemical absorbency and rapid effect
  • Easy manual application and long-term storage stability
  • Provided with MSDS document; NSN: 6505-27-014-1446
