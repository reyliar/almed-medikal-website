Product Details

To meet the standards that drive the market, the established standards for configuration and reporting include the following:
ISO 14644-1:2015 - Cleanrooms and associated controlled environments

EU GMP Annex 1 (2008 and 2022) – Good Manufacturing Practices: Manufacture of Sterile Medical Products

Whether used independently or as part of a comprehensive, easy-to-use monitoring system, the AeroTrak™+ Portable Particle Counter A100 Series meets your needs.

A single device does it all: classification, monitoring, filtering, scanning, gas testing, and root cause analysis
High-visibility status indicators—LED light bar visible up to 20 meters / 360°
Access user controls for GMP compliance
Sample the correct volume of gas with built-in gas correction factors
Automatic ISO 14644-1 and GMP (EU GMP and China GMP) reporting and instrument workflow with the included TrakPro™ Lite Safe Software
Filter test and research mode (both audible and visual)
ISO 21501-4 calibration compliance
WiFi®-enabled connectivity
Industry-leading 5-year laser warranty
Purpose-built—easy-to-clean, stainless steel housing
Facilitate integration with third-party systems via 4–20 mA analog particle data output and environmental sensor input options
Multi-instrument reporting supported by the latest TrakPro™ Lite Safe Software
