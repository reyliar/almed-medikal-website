Technical Details

Pegase™ 7 is a portable air and surface disinfection nebulizer that integrates a controlled vortex venturi system with a 20-meter range, producing a dry, cold mist that eliminates the risk of corrosion. Compact, robust, and ergonomic, Pegase™ 7 features an automatic timer (with 90- or 300-second settings before diffusion begins) and a progressive audible alarm to simplify daily operations.Both lightweight (5.3 kg) and powerful, PEGASE™ 7 is highly portable and economical (dosage of 6 ml/m³). It can treat up to 165 m³ in a single cycle.
Pegase™ 7 is designed for use with SPRAY OXY PAE™, a ready-to-use 7.4% pure hydrogen peroxide disinfectant. Its ABS casing makes it easy to clean and highly impact-resistant.Diffusion of SPRAY OXY PAE™ can be initiated at the end of the day or when the area is vacated; the unit operates autonomously, requiring no operator presence in the room.
The SPRAY OXY PAE™ disinfectant contains no organic residues. The disinfection cycle concludes with the solution decomposing completely into free oxygen and water, leaving no surface residue. SPRAY OXY PAE™ is a biocide containing 7.4% hydrogen peroxide, compliant with European Regulation 2009/107/EC. It is also safe for use on surfaces that come into direct contact with food.
