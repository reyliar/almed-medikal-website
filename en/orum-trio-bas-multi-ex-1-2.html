Orum Trio.Bas Multiﬂex 1+2

Product Details

The MULTIFLEX 1+2 air sampler and its satellite units are constructed from AISI 316 grade stainless steel.
This air sampler is specifically designed for customers who conduct extensive monitoring across various environments—often involving significant personnel rotation—while adhering to quality standards and QM/GMP requirements.
Our primary clients are in the pharmaceutical, cleanroom, and biotechnology industries.
A barcode module—utilizing a Bluetooth scanner (barcode reader)—automatically records the operator, location, and culture plates used for sampling. Data collected by the barcode reader is transmitted directly to the device. This solution is ideal for users who already employ culture plates with standard or 2-D barcodes (QR/Quick Response Codes). Collected data is transferred from the air sampler to a PC or laptop via Bluetooth; this requires the installation of dedicated software.
Data can also be transferred via Bluetooth between the air sampler and a smartphone or tablet (Android) before being moved to a PC or laptop.
Operation is possible in either manual or automatic mode.
Data can also be transferred via cable; this is beneficial for companies restricted from using wireless transmission due to internal policies.
The battery is charged using a power cable connected directly to the air sampler.
An airflow rate of 200 L/min reduces both operator time and sampling duration.
This air sampler enables the monitoring of separate cleanrooms using a single external control unit. Since the satellite units remain permanently inside each cleanroom, the risk of human contamination is reduced.
The use of sterile "Daily Shift" aspiration heads reduces the risk of contamination.
The ability to use 2 or 3 different aspiration heads allows for the simultaneous use of 2 or 3 different culture media, or enables sampling BEFORE (during rest periods), DURING (while operational), and AFTER each process cycle.
