Orum Trio. Compressed Air Bacterial Sampling

Product Details

TRIO.GAS guarantees that the air coming into contact with the product in sterile or aseptic production facilities (e.g., cleanrooms) is free from contamination.
Our primary customers are in the pharmaceutical, cleanroom, food, and dairy industries.
The system complies with ISO Standards 8573-7 and 14698-1.
Before passing through the TRIO.BAS air sampler, the airflow from the pressurized supply is regulated by an autoclavable valve.
All sampling data is transferred via Bluetooth to a tablet/smartphone or PC (if using the TRIO.BAS air sampler) using dedicated software that complies with GMP and GLP standards.
When TRIO.GAS is used with the TRIO.BAS air sampler, the timing is controlled by the air sampler's software.
When TRIO.GAS is used with the ASPI Gas Chamber, the test is manual, and the timing is controlled by a timer.

Technical Details

Constructed entirely from AISI 316 grade stainless steel
Fully autoclavable without requiring disassembly
Air sampling flow rate100 liters/min
Sampling head compatible with 90 mm Petri dishes or 55 mm contact plates
Compliant with ISO 14698-1, EN 17141, ISO 8537-7, and FDA standards
