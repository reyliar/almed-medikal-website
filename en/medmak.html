MEDMAK Swab Folding & Packaging

The MEDMAK LXZ-50 / LXZ-75 / LXZ-100 single swab transfer unit is an auxiliary unit that provides controlled feeding of individual swabs from the swab folding machine into the adapted packaging machine. It operates with a PLC-controlled, vacuum-assisted, fully automatic principle.

Model Serisi LXZ-50 / 75 / 100
Kapasite 1 – 5 swab/paket
Transfer Vakum Destekli
Kontrol PLC Otomatik Sistem
Technical Specifications

MEDMAK Technical Details

The transfer unit acts as a bridge between the swab folding machine and the packaging machine; it receives individual swabs through a vacuum-assisted system, groups them in selectable quantities, and feeds them to the packaging machine in a controlled manner.
