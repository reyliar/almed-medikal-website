Biological & Chemical Sampling Kit

Portable hardcase kit designed for sampling from air, water, soil, surface, material, and living/environmental sources in areas threatened by biological and chemical warfare agents, and establishing a secure transport chain. NATO Stock No: 6665-27-050-6881.

Key Features

  • Provides a sampling setup for air, water, soil, objects, surfaces, and materials.
  • Biological and chemical tubes are classified with their own color codes.
  • Sterile tubes are suitable for collecting and transporting bacterial and viral samples.
  • All materials are secured and labeled inside a waterproof case.
  • Includes sampling equipment made of stainless steel and PP material.
  • Usage forms, labels, and packaging materials support the identification process.

Areas of Use

  • Field CBRN incident-site investigation
  • Secure sample transfer to the laboratory

  • SIBCRA-focused sampling processes
