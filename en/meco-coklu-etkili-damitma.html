MECO Multi-Effect Distillation

Product Details

STANDARD FEATURES
The unit is fully piped, wired, and mounted on skids at the factory
- Fully functional FAT
- Reduces installation costs and startup time
Vertical natural circulation evaporators
- Reduces scaling effects
- No circulation pumps or spray nozzles required
- Straight, annealed tubes in the evaporator
- Individual tubes can be replaced instead of replacing the entire tube bundle
- No residual stresses or surface quality issues commonly found in U-tubes
- Dual condensers
- Eliminates potential contamination between feedwater and cooling water
- Hot standby option
- Better response to distilled product demand
- Elimination of biological growth
Plumbing installation and connections
PID loop control of WFI temperature
Optional PID loop control of feedwater level and initial pressure
PLC-based controls
EQUIPMENT DETAILS
- Double-tube plate heat exchangers
- Inclined tubes and low-point drains
- Minimal dead legs
- 316/316L SS process-contact components
- 25 Ra standard process-contact surfaces
- Premium valves and fittings
- Stainless steel air lines
- Stainless steel hardware
- Mirror-finish stainless steel cladding
